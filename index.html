Our Mission

BuggedOut404 is dedicated to providing accessible and engaging STEM education resources. We believe that learning should be an adventure, and our kits are designed to spark curiosity and foster a love for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Our mission is to make STEM education fun and accessible for everyone, regardless of their background or location. Through hands-on projects and innovative learning materials, we're building the next generation of problem solvers and innovators.
